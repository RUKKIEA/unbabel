Unbabel Partnerships

Unbabel works hand-in-hand with some of the world's largest BPOs and outsourcing providers. They bring scale and operational expertise, and we provide our AI-powered, human-refined machine learning translation technology. Together, we've created custom, transformative, never-before-seen multilingual support initiatives for some of the biggest brands worldwide.

We work with our partners to deliver exceptional multilingual support and custom translation solutions around the world. Together we help brands…

Save money

by consolidating and moving contact centers to lower-cost locations.

Boost efficiency

by combining augmented machine translation with human editors for real-time, high-quality translations that work anywhere.

Delight customers

by providing instant, authentic support in their native language.

Danny Kuivenhoven,

Head of Digital Transformation

For our customers

We work together with you and your BPO partner to help you achieve your customer support goals. We work closely to develop a translation process that is seamless and makes sense for your bottom-line.

Hit the ground translating

Unbabel easily plugs into the customer service platforms you’re already using, such as Zendesk, Salesforce, Intercom, and Freshworks, serving as acting like an instant, invisible translation layer between agents and customers. So you can offer multilingual support to your target languages across multiple channels, including chat, email, and FAQs.
